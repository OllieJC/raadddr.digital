RAADDDR Framework

The complete lifecycle for modern digital products: Research to Run.

Methodology Breakdown

  • R - Research Conducting primary research to understand user needs, behaviours, and motivations before any solution is defined. Roles: User Researcher, Service Designer
  • A - Analyse Interpreting complex data and business requirements to inform strategic decisions and process improvements. Roles: Business Analyst, Data Analyst
  • A - Architect Designing secure, scalable, and resilient technical systems that integrate seamlessly with existing infrastructure. Roles: Technical Architect, Solution Architect
  • D - Design Creating accessible, intuitive interfaces and clear content that guide users through complex services. Roles: Interaction Designer, Content Designer
  • D - Develop Building the underlying code and systems using modern standards to ensure software is robust, testable, and maintainable. Roles: Software Engineer, Frontend Developer
  • D - Deliver Leading agile teams to deliver value continuously, removing blockers and ensuring the team focuses on the right priorities. Roles: Delivery Manager, Product Manager
  • R - Run Managing the ongoing health, security, and availability of live services, ensuring they continue to meet user needs 24/7. Roles: Site Reliability Engineer, DevOps Engineer
